STUDY TITLE: UTTERANCE INTERPRETATION

Study Purpose

In this study, you will rate how likely different statements are. The statements will be about utterances (i.e., what people have said) in short dialogues/scenarios.

Participant Tasks

You will be presented with a series of short scenarios. For each scenario, you will move a slider to indicate how likely you believe a given statement to be. You may also optionally provide a brief written explanation of your response. We have carefully designed each question to not contain any sensitive or personal content.

Risks and Benefits

This experiment involves no known risks or discomfort to you beyond the risks of everyday life, nor is it expected to be of any direct benefit to you.

Compensation

US$15.00/hr, pro-rated to the median amount of time taken to complete the task.

Data Collection

We will record your Prolific ID and the fact that you are a native English speaker and situated in either the US, the UK or Canada. Your Prolific ID will be stored privately and deleted one year after the study has taken place.

Data Storage and Use

Your anonymous responses may be uploaded to a public GitHub repository for research purposes. Your anonymous responses (excluding Prolific IDs) may be shared on a public GitHub repository for research purposes, provided you have given your consent below. Data will be stored indefinitely.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is voluntary. You may withdraw from the study at any time. Compensation will be awarded on a case by case basis.
